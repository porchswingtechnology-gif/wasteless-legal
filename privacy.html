Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

WasteLess ("we", "our", "the app") is a receipt-scanning and meal-planning iOS app published by Mindforge Learning LLC. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We try to keep this honest and short.

What we collect

  • Sign in with Apple: When you sign in, Apple shares your name and a relay email address with us. You can also use the app as a guest, in which case we receive no identifying information.
  • Photos you scan: Receipt and pantry photos you take in the app are sent to our backend server for processing by an AI service (Anthropic's Claude). Photos are processed transiently to extract grocery items and are not stored long-term on our servers.
  • App data: Your pantry items, meal plans, dietary preferences, and waste-tracking data are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. We do not upload this data to our servers.
  • Subscription status: Apple's StoreKit handles all payments. We see whether you are a Pro subscriber, but we never see your payment details.

How we use it

  • To provide the core service — extract grocery items from receipts, identify pantry contents, and generate meal plans.
  • To respect your dietary preferences and allergies when generating recipes.
  • To enforce free-tier scan limits and unlock Pro features for active subscribers.
  • To send local notifications you've enabled (expiry reminders, weekly meal plan reminder).

Who we share it with

  • Anthropic: Photos and pantry data are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for AI processing. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their privacy policy.
  • Spoonacular: Pantry item names may be sent to Spoonacular when generating recipe suggestions. See their terms.
  • Apple: Subscriptions, sign-in, and notifications go through Apple's standard iOS APIs.
  • We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use it for marketing.

Data retention

Local app data lives on your device until you delete the app or sign out. Photos sent to our backend are processed and discarded — we keep no copies. Account information (Sign in with Apple identifiers) is retained as long as your account is active.

Your rights

You can sign out at any time from the Preferences tab. You can revoke Apple's sign-in access in iOS Settings → Apple ID → Sign-In with Apple. Deleting the app removes all local data. To request deletion of any server-side data tied to your account, email us at the address below.

Children

WasteLess is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date at the top and call out significant changes in-app or via email if you've signed in.
